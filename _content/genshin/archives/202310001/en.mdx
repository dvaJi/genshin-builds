## How to Use Anemoculus

### Enhance Your Statue of the Seven

Anemoculus, the precious collectibles of Mondstadt, hold the key to enhancing the Statues of the Seven. As you offer them to these statues, you'll increase your stamina, earn Primogems, and gain Adventure EXP.

Discover all 65 Anemoculus scattered throughout Mondstadt.

**The Mystery of the Extra Anemoculus**

Even after powering up the Statues of the Seven, you may find yourself with one mysterious Anemoculus left. Its purpose remains shrouded in mystery.

## Anemoculus Resonance Stone Gadget

### Finding Missing Anemoculi

Version 1.1 introduced a game-changing tool for Anemoculus hunters.

### Unlocking the Anemoculus Resonance Stone

To obtain the blueprint for the Anemoculus Resonance Stone, achieve City Reputation Level 2 in Mondstadt.

### Crafting the Anemoculus Resonance Stone

| Material       | Quantity |
| -------------- | -------- |
| Dandelion Seed | 5        |
| Cecilia        | 5        |
| Crystal Chunk  | 1        |

These materials are essential for crafting the Anemoculus Resonance Stone. Dandelion Seeds and Cecilia can only be found in Mondstadt.

### Activating the Anemoculus Resonance Stone

Once crafted, equip and use the item from your inventory. It will unveil an approximate location on your map where an undiscovered Anemoculus awaits.
